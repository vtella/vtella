Sales Commission on Sales/Invoice/Register Payment based on Configuration Odoo

Want to provide commission to your Salesman/Manager for specific Product, Product Categories or based on profit margin now there is no such kind of features in default Odoo so here you can have it.
This App is calculates sales commission when invoice is get paid it calculates based on configurations of sales commission policy defined such as Partner, Product categories, Product and profit margin.

Sales Commission Configuration

You have to do some configuration after installing this module. First of all you have to give rights user in order to use functionality of this module will be visible to that user.


Sales Commission Conditions Configuration

Here you can configure based on which condition related sales persons commission will be calculate. you can calculate commission in three ways :
Commission based on sales order:
In this condition when sale order is confirmed at that time the comission of related salesperson or manager is calculated
Commission based on invoice:
In this condition when invoice is validated at that time the comission of related salesperson or manager is calculated
Commission based on payment:
In this condition when invoice is paid at that time the comission of related salesperson or manager is calculated

Commission based on sales order:

When you want to give commission to the slaes person while the sale order is being confirmed you can select this condition. you can find sales commission lines under tha tab of sales commisions on sales order form.


Commission based on invoice:

When you want to give commission to the slaes person while the invoice is being validated you can select this condition. you can find sales commission lines under tha tab of sales commisions on invoice form


Commission based on payment:

When you want to give commission to the slaes person while the invoice is being paid you can select this condition. you can find sales commission lines under tha tab of sales commisions on invoice form


Sales commission menu

You can find menu of sales commission under configuration menu of sales.


Types of Commission provided

You can defined three types of commission from this module

  • Standard Commission
  • Partner based Commission
  • Product/ Product Category/ Margin Based Commission

Standard Commission

Standard commission is a basic type of commission you need to just add the percentage of commission you want to give to the selected sales person based on total sale order under that sales user.


Partner based Commission

In Partner based Commission you can define two diffrent percentage of sales commission based on category of partner weather partner is Affiliated Partner or Non-Affiliated Partner.A partner can be Affiliated Partner or Non-Affiliated Partner not both.




Product/ Product Category/ Margin Based Commission

In Product/ Product Category/ Margin Based Commission you need to define based on which field and what product category or product subcategory you want to define sales commission.

Calculation based on Product:
If you select calculation based on Product the sales commission will be given on the unit sale price of that product.
Calculation based on Product Category:
If you select calculation based on Product the sales commission will be given on the unit sale price of that product category.
Calculation based on Margin Based:
If you select calculation based on Product the sales commission will be given on based on the margin between tha sale price and cost price of that product sold.


You can also specify on which factor you want to give sales commission


Fix Price

Sales commission will be applied on fix price given by the user


Margin Price

In margin price sales commission will be calculate according to margin parmeter given.


Commission Exception

With this option you have to just give the percentage of sales commission
